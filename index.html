Thu chu cai

Huong phat trien ky thuat

  • Am thanh mien Nam: ghi am giong that (MP3/WebM), phat bang HTMLAudioElement hoac Howler.js (preload, gapless, mobile).
  • Du lieu noi dung: file JSON (chu cai, tu, URL anh, URL am thanh) de them sua khong can doi code.
  • Hinh anh: SVG/PNG to, nut to (toi thieu ~48px cham), vung bam rong — phu hop be ~15 thang.
  • Random: chon ngau nhien N muc tu JSON, tranh trung trong mot vong; co the gioi han bo chu can hoc.
  • TTS du phong: speechSynthesis + vi-VN khi chua co file ghi am (chat luong & giong vung mien khong on dinh).
  • PWA / offline: Service Worker cache anh + am de choi khong mang.
  • Khuyen nghi: am luong vua, tranh nhap nhay manh; cha me giam sat khi dung tai nghe.
